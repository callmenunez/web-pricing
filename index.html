Pricing details

The “Domain Included” and the “Domain Only” domain discounts are valid for a single one (1) year domain name registration at Web.com and only for new registrations (not renewals) of generally available (not premium) domain names with the .com, .net, .org, .info, and .biz extensions. After the applicable promotional period, then-current renewal rates shall apply (currently, $15.99 for a single one (1) year domain name registration and $31.98 for a single two (2) year domain name registration). Domain names are registered on a first-come, first-served basis. There are no guarantees or warranties that the domain name will be available when you go to our website or will be accepted by the associated registry. The rights to the domain will be granted to the customer 30 days after purchase and a second payment has been made to Web.com. This offer may not be combined with any other offer. All pricing subject to change at the sole discretion of Web.com. Customer may cancel prior to the end of the promotional period or at any time thereafter by contacting Web.com Customer Service at (800) 862-8921. See our Services Agreement for additional details.
